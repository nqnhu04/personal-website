Hello there! I'm Nhu, a passionate 3rd year student pursuing Computer Science. I started my programming journey in high school with Python and Java but have really taken it to the next step in college. My current interests lie in web development and AI. I particularly enjoy web development for its artistic frontend side and its technical backend side. Through the organization, I've partnered with California Southern Edison, Google, NFL, and more to come onto campus and host technical events for our students. My time as president is my proudest and most grateful achievement at CSULB.
